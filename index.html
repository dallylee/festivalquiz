When do 2026 festival tickets usually go on sale?
Most major festivals release tickets between October 2025 and February 2026. Sign up for festival newsletters and set calendar reminders. Popular festivals like Tomorrowland and Wacken often sell out within hours!
Which 2026 European festivals are best for solo travelers?
Roskilde (Denmark), Sziget (Hungary), and Way Out West (Sweden) have particularly welcoming communities for solo festival-goers. Join festival Facebook groups to find travel buddies and camping companions.
Which 2026 festivals are better for families with children?
WOMAD, Latitude, Green Man, and Electric Picnic all have dedicated family camping areas and kids' activities. These festivals offer a more relaxed atmosphere with diverse programming beyond just music.
How early should I book accommodation for big 2026 festivals?
For city festivals (Primavera, Wireless), book accommodation as soon as you have tickets - hotels fill up fast. For camping festivals, standard camping is usually included, but glamping options should be booked 2-3 months ahead.
